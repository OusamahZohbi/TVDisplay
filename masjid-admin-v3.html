How to set up

1. Open the display link on any browser (Chrome recommended) on your display screen.
2. Press F11 to enter fullscreen mode.
3. The screen will auto-refresh overnight and pick up new content within 3 minutes of any change.
4. For best results, keep the browser open 24/7 and disable screen sleep.
