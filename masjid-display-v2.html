🌟 Best Day of the Week
The Blessed Friday
يَوْمُ الْجُمُعَة الْمُبَارَك
خَيْرُ يَوْمٍ طَلَعَتْ عَلَيْهِ الشَّمْسُ يَوْمُ الْجُمُعَةِ
"The best day on which the sun rises is Friday. On it Adam was created, on it he entered Paradise, and on it he was expelled from it."
Abu Hurayrah (RA) · Sahih Muslim 854
🕌 Jumu'ah Prayer
1:30 PM
📍 Lurnea Community Hall  •  Khutbah at 1:15 PM
⭐ Sunnah Acts of Friday
1
Perform ghusl before Jumu'ah prayer
2
Wear your best clothes and apply perfume
3
Recite Surah Al-Kahf in full
4
Send abundant salawat on the Prophet ﷺ
5
Arrive early to the masjid and sit close
6
Seek the blessed hour when du'a is accepted
7
Pray 2 rak'ahs upon entering the masjid
🤲 Salawat on the Prophet ﷺ
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ
"O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad and upon the family of Muhammad."
